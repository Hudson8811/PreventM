Prevent™Care — Online Urgent Car
What it does and what it does not.
does
Gives you cheap access to board certified doctors nationwide, 24hrs a day, 7 days a week for less than a dollar a day.
doesn’t
Replace your primary care physician, cover life-threatening visits to the ER or replace specialized doctor visits required to prescribe certain medications.
Prevent Care is great for addressing "Common Acute Cold illnesses & Symptoms" that can be diagnosed by a licensed medical practitioner and should be thought of as your digital urgent care, which in times like these can be helpful in understanding the severity of certain symptoms and whether or not emergency care may be required
